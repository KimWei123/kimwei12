Our Story

Our Mission

Transconverge was founded with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between the world's most beloved pet brands and the rapidly growing Chinese pet market. We recognized a demand for high-quality, authentic pet products that were not always readily available locally.

By combining our expertise in international trade with cutting-edge AI technology and China's booming instant retail infrastructure, we have created a seamless supply chain that delivers premium pet products to consumers across China quickly and reliably.

Our vision is to become the most trusted partner for both international pet brands looking to enter China and Chinese consumers seeking the very best for their furry family members.
